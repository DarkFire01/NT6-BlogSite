Who am I?

I am contributor to the ReactOS project; I've implemented features such as xHCI and UEFI support But I have an insanely large facination with modern windows. To those who don't know ReactOS is a open source operating system designed to be binary compatible with the Windows NT line of OSes be essentially being a clone of windows made with reverse engineering. Currently targetting NT5.2

So what is this?

This blog is a place to document all of the interesting information I find out about how modern windows works. My goal is to get modern software support into ReactOS and understanding a large plethera of NT6+ APIs.
